About

I am a software developer with over 15 years of experience, specialized in backend development, with solid skills also in DevOps and frontend.

I have worked mainly on enterprise projects, developing a good ability to tackle complex systems and legacy code, which I like to improve through targeted refactoring to increase readability, testability and maintainability.

I pay great attention to software quality, maintaining control of code architecture to produce well-organized systems that are easy to evolve. I work with a long-term vision, building systems that are easy to extend and evolve over time.

In recent years I have started sharing my experience by participating as a speaker at conferences for local developers.
